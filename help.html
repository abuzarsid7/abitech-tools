üîê Password Generator

Create strong, secure, random passwords with customizable options. Passwords are generated entirely in your browser and never sent anywhere.

How to Use:

  1. Visit the Password Generator tool
  2. Set your desired password length (typically 12-32 characters)
  3. Choose which character types to include:
    • Uppercase letters (A-Z)
    • Lowercase letters (a-z)
    • Numbers (0-9)
    • Special characters (!@#$%^&* etc.)
  4. Click the "Generate Password" button
  5. Copy the generated password and use it for your account

Best Practices:

  • Use passwords of at least 12-16 characters for better security
  • Include a mix of all character types (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols)
  • Use a unique password for each account
  • Store passwords securely in a password manager
  • Never share passwords or write them down in plain text
  • Change passwords regularly, especially for important accounts

Security Notes:

100% Private: All passwords are generated using your browser's cryptographic random number generator. They are never transmitted over the internet or stored on our servers. Your passwords are completely private and secure.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! All tools process data entirely in your browser. Nothing you type or generate is sent to our servers. Your data stays on your device.

Yes! All tools are 100% free with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or subscriptions.

Once the tool page is loaded, most functionality works offline since processing happens in your browser. However, you need an initial internet connection to load the page.

All modern browsers are supported: Google Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Microsoft Edge, and Opera. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

üîß Technical Support

Tool Not Working?

If you're experiencing issues, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Refresh the page (press F5 or Ctrl+R / Cmd+R)
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Try using a different browser
  4. Disable browser extensions that might interfere (ad blockers, script blockers)
  5. Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
  6. Check if your browser is up to date
