Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Abitech Pros Tools ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website abitechpros.com.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

Privacy by Design: All our tools operate entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. This means:

  • No Text Data Storage: Any text you enter into the Text Counter is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • No Password Storage: Passwords generated by our Password Generator are created entirely in your browser and are never sent to or stored on our servers.
  • No QR Code Data Storage: QR codes are generated locally in your browser. The content you encode is never transmitted to us.
  • No User Accounts: We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our tools.
  • No Form Data Collection: We do not collect any data you input into any of our tools.

2. Information We May Collect

While we don't collect data from the tools themselves, we may collect limited information through standard web technologies:

Analytics Data

We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site. This may include:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Geographic location (country/city level only)
  • Referring website
  • Date and time of visit

Log Files

Like most websites, our web server automatically collects certain information in log files. This information includes:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
  • Browser type
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Date and time stamp
  • Referring/exit pages

3. How We Use Information

The limited anonymous information we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website
  • Improve our tools and user experience
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends
  • Detect and prevent technical issues
  • Improve website performance and security

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device.

Types of Cookies We May Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our website.

5. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and services to facilitate our website, such as:

  • Analytics Providers: To analyze website usage
  • Hosting Services: To host our website
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN): To deliver website content efficiently

These third parties have access only to anonymous data necessary to perform their tasks and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures designed to protect the limited information we collect. Our website uses HTTPS encryption to secure data transmission between your browser and our servers.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request information about any data we may have about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your data

Since we collect minimal anonymous data and no personal information from tool usage, most of these rights are not applicable. However, you can always contact us with any privacy concerns.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

9. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information, including any anonymous analytics data, may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. By using our website, you consent to such transfers.

11. Do Not Track Signals

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) signals. If your browser has DNT enabled, we will honor that preference where technically feasible with our analytics providers.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Website: abitechpros.com
  • Email: contact@abitechpros.com

Summary

Your privacy is our priority. Our tools process everything locally in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our tools. We only collect anonymous analytics to improve our service. Your passwords, text, and QR codes stay on your device.
